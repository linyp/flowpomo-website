Powerful Features

FlowPomo provides professional Pomodoro features to help you work and study more efficiently

Pomodoro Timer

Classic Pomodoro Technique: 25 minutes of focused work, 5 minutes of rest. Fully customizable work, break durations and cycles.

Dynamic Island

Real-time timer progress on iPhone Dynamic Island and Lock Screen. Check progress without unlocking your phone.

Health Sync

Automatically sync your focus time to Apple Health as Mindful Minutes. Track your wellness journey.

Calendar Sync

Completed focus sessions are automatically added to your calendar. Review and analyze your time allocation.

Focus Guard

Block distracting apps during focus sessions. Stay fully concentrated on your work without interruptions.

Custom Tags

Create tags for different tasks. Categorize and track your time investment across projects.

Data Export

Export your focus session data to CSV format. Analyze your productivity trends with your favorite tools.
