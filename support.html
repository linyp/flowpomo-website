Q: How do I restore my purchase?
A: Go to Settings, tap "Upgrade to Pro", then select "Restore Purchases".

Q: Why isn't Health Sync working?
A: Please make sure you've granted FlowPomo write permission in iOS Settings > Privacy & Security > Health.

Q: How can I export my data?
A: FlowPomo Pro users can use the export feature on the Statistics page to export data as a CSV file.

Q: How do I change timer duration?
A: Go to Settings and adjust the work duration, short break, long break and cycles to your preference.
